# About Me

I am a Computational Biologist specializing in deep learning for sequence–structure prediction, protein language models, and HPC-scale training pipelines. I hold an M.Sc. in Computational Biology and Bioinformatics from Georg-August-Universität Göttingen (GPA 1.6) and am looking for a PhD position where I can build on this foundation in a computationally rigorous group.

At the **Söding Lab** (Max Planck Institute for Multidisciplinary Sciences), I developed **PMBind** — a transformer-based pMHC binding predictor using custom 2D masked self-attention, protein language models (ESM), and transfer learning to under-represented alleles. I also contributed as second author to **PMGen** (preprint: [10.1101/2025.11.14.688404](https://doi.org/10.1101/2025.11.14.688404)), an integrated framework for pMHC structure modeling and neoantigen generation.

As a Research Assistant at the **Hauschild Lab** (UMG / Justus Liebig University Giessen), I work on the **FAIrPaCT consortium**, building multi-modal federated learning pipelines for pancreatic cancer survival modeling from somatic mutations (MAF), RNA expression, and clinical variables across distributed clinical sites. I have published two PyPI packages from this work: [FedPanod](https://pypi.org/project/FedPanod/) and [FRSF4POD](https://pypi.org/project/FRSF4POD/).

Recent independent highlights:
- 🥇 **Kaggle Gold Medal** — Stanford RNA 3D Folding Part 2: built a pipeline combining homology modeling, MSA-guided folding, and deep learning for 3D coordinate prediction
- **bayesian-playgrounds** — interactive webpage for teaching Bayesian and simulation-based inference
- **CAFA 6 Protein Function Prediction** — GNNs, BioBERT, MMseqs2, ESM-3

I was awarded the **Niedersachsenstipendium** (State of Lower Saxony graduate scholarship) in 2025 and received two Erasmus+ BIP travel grants.

**Technical skills:**
- **Programming**: Python (TensorFlow, Keras, PyTorch, scikit-learn, Pandas, NumPy, BioPython, Biotite, RDKit), R, Bash, LaTeX, TypeScript, React
- **ML / Data Science**: Deep Learning, Representation Learning, Transfer Learning, Federated Learning, NLP, Simulation-based Inference (Bayesian), Survival Analysis
- **Bioinformatics**: RNA secondary and 3D structure prediction, template-based modeling, sequence–structure alignment (FoldMason, MAFFT), protein language models (ESM), multi-omics integration, genomic variant processing (MAF/VCF), RNA-seq
- **Infrastructure**: Docker, Git/GitLab CI/CD, HPC/SLURM, Conda/Mamba, Linux

I am fluent in English (C1), German (B1), and Persian (native).
